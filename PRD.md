# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement
Jack Laughlin is a Business Analytics student at the University of Iowa graduating in May 2027 with a 3.80 GPA. He is targeting data analyst roles in the short term and plans to attend law school to become an attorney.

A traditional resume does not fully communicate Jack’s personality, leadership experience, and technical projects. This landing page will serve as a centralized, dynamic professional presence that allows recruiters and admissions representatives to better understand his skills, experience, and career direction.

---

## 02 — Target Audience
The primary audience includes:
- Data internship recruiters
- Law recruiters
- Law school admissions representatives

These users are primarily located in the Midwest and represent organizations such as:
- Companies hiring data analysts (various industries)
- Law firms
- Law schools

When visiting the page, they are looking for:
- Clear demonstration of technical and analytical skills
- Evidence of leadership and discipline (athletics, awards, teamwork)
- A sense of personality and professional direction beyond a resume

---

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | How We Measure It |
|------|------------------|
| Generate recruiter/admissions interest | 10 inbound messages within 30–60 days |
| Secure interviews | 5 interviews within 30–60 days |
| Drive job/internship applications | 20 applications submitted |
